Getting Started

What is this Online Vehicle-Pass Registration System?
This is a convenient and efficient online platform designed to streamline the process of registering your vehicle for campus access. Say goodbye to paper forms and long queues!
Who needs to register their vehicle?
All students, faculty, and staff who intend to park their vehicles on campus are required to register through this system.
How do I create an account?
Simply click on the "Create Free Account" button on the homepage. You will be guided through a simple registration process where you'll need to provide your corporate email and follow the on-screen instructions.
What information will I need to provide during registration?
You will typically need to provide details such as your name, your affiliation with the institution (student, faculty, or staff), your vehicle's make, model, color, and license plate number. You may also be asked for your department or student ID.
Is there a fee to register my vehicle?
Please refer to the institution's parking policies for information regarding any applicable registration fees. Details about fees, if any, will be clearly outlined during the registration process.

Managing Your Vehicle Pass

How long is my vehicle pass valid?
The validity period of your vehicle pass will depend on your affiliation with the institution (e.g., academic year for students, employment period for staff). You will be notified of the expiry date through the system.
Can I register more than one vehicle?
The system typically allows the registration of multiple vehicles per user. However, please check the institution's parking policy for any limitations on the number of registered vehicles allowed at one time.
How do I update my vehicle information?
You can easily update your vehicle information by logging into your account and navigating to the "Manage Vehicles" section. Follow the prompts to edit or add new vehicle details.
What if I get a temporary vehicle or a rental car?
The system usually allows you to add a temporary vehicle. Look for a section like "Add Temporary Vehicle" in your account. You will likely need to specify the start and end dates for the temporary vehicle's access.
What if I lose my vehicle pass or have an issue with it?
Please contact the campus security or parking services department immediately. Their contact information can usually be found on the "Contact Us" page of this system or the institution's website.

Technical Support

What if I forget my password?
Click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. You will be guided through a password recovery process, usually involving your registered email address.
I'm having trouble registering my vehicle. What should I do?
Please refer to the "FAQ" or "Contact Us" section for troubleshooting tips and contact information for technical support. You can reach out via the contact form or the provided email address.
Is my personal information secure?
Yes, we take the security of your personal information very seriously. Our system employs industry-standard security measures to protect your data. Please refer to our Privacy Policy for more details.
What browsers are supported by this system?
This system is designed to work seamlessly with most modern web browsers, including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. For the best experience, please ensure your browser is up to date.

Still have questions?

If you couldn't find the answer to your question, our support team is here to help you with any inquiries you might have.
